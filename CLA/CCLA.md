# Corporate Contributor License Agreement (CCLA)

Version 1.0

This Agreement is between the organization identified below ("Company") and the
project maintainers for this repository and its successor names/URLs ("Project").

By signing this CCLA, Company agrees:

## 1. Purpose

Company permits its employees/contractors listed by Company to contribute to the
Project under the terms below.

## 2. Inbound = Outbound Grant

For contributions Company owns or controls, Company grants the Project a
non-exclusive, worldwide, perpetual, irrevocable, royalty-free license to use,
reproduce, modify, distribute, and sublicense those contributions only under the
same outbound licensing model already used by this repository, as documented in
`LICENSES.md` and file/folder notices.

This CCLA does not transfer ownership and does not grant broader relicensing
rights than the repository's stated outbound license model.

## 3. No Additional Patent Terms in This CLA

This CCLA does not add a separate patent license grant beyond what applicable
file licenses already provide.

## 4. Company Representations

Company represents that:

- The signatory has authority to bind Company.
- Company will authorize only contributors it has rights to authorize.
- Company will notify maintainers when authorized contributor status changes.

## 5. Identity and Accountability

- Anonymous and pseudonymous contributor identities are not accepted.
- A government-issued legal name is not required.
- Authorized contributors must use a consistent human name and reachable email.

## 6. Public Record

Company understands that contributions and related signature records are public
and retained as part of project history.

## 7. Warranty Disclaimer

Except as required by law, contributions are provided "as is", without warranties.
